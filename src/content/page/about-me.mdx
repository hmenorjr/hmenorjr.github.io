Hey, my name’s Herman. I’m a General Technologist from the Philippines, currently living in the Åland Islands, Finland. I’ve been creating websites since 2007. My journey in the technology industry has led me to become a DevOps Engineer, but my heart is still in creating websites.

My interest started in high school when the internet was just emerging. HTML, CSS and JavaScript are my main technologies, and I built websites using either Microsoft’s Notepad or DreamWeaver.

During college, I had extra allowance because students all over the campus were sending me requests to customize their Friendster and Multiply profiles. If you don’t know what those are, you’re probably too young.

In 2006, I started working as a Junior Web Developer using JavaEE. From there, my journey led me to become a Back-end Developer / Software Engineer.

I’m starting a new venture because I noticed that many small and medium-sized family businesses want to have their own websites but can’t afford a custom-built one. Although there are free options available, they still prefer to have their own unique websites.

I found a solution to this problem: a subscription-based web development service with unlimited requests. I am currently helping businesses in the Nordic region and Europe.
